I drew a picture that I intend to use as my new Gravatar. 
It's a Superman logo with a "Z" instead of an "S", since I like my first initial so much. 
Here it is: ![Turtle image](http://imgur.com/1b3QQKV)

My code was brute force. I have a couple of for loops to make curves but for the most part I "freehanded" this picture by using Turtle's ```goto()``` method. Here's my code:

```import turtle```

```wn = turtle.Screen()````

```wn.bgcolor('navy')```

```zuperman = turtle.Turtle()```

```zuperman.color('red')```

```zuperman.speed(0)```

```i = 24```

```zuperman.pensize(i)```                

```zuperman.penup()```

```zuperman.fillcolor('gold')```

```zuperman.begin_fill()```

```zuperman.goto(0, -200)```

```zuperman.pendown()```

```zuperman.goto(-195, 75)```

```zuperman.goto(-150, 195)```

```zuperman.goto(150, 195)```

```zuperman.goto(195, 75)```

```zuperman.goto(0, -200)```

```zuperman.end_fill()```

```zuperman.penup()```

```zuperman.goto(-150, 192)```

```zuperman.pendown()```

```zuperman.color('black')```

```zuperman.fillcolor('red')```

```zuperman.begin_fill()```

```zuperman.pensize(i/4)```

```zuperman.goto(150, 192)```

```zuperman.goto(150, 170)```

```zuperman.goto(0, -20)```

```for i in range(90):```

    ```zuperman.forward(1)```
    
   ```zuperman.left(0.5)```
    
```zuperman.right(45)```

```zuperman.forward(70)```

```zuperman.goto(70, -95)```

```zuperman.goto(-70,-95)```

```zuperman.goto(-140, 0)```

```zuperman.goto(0, 140)```

```zuperman.left(180)```

```for i in range(90):```

    ```zuperman.forward(1)```
    
    ```zuperman.left(0.5)```
    
```zuperman.right(45)```

```zuperman.goto(-150, 50)```

```zuperman.goto(-150, 192)```

```zuperman.end_fill()```

```zuperman.hideturtle()```

```wn.exitonclick()```
